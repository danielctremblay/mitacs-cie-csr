1.    Species included in these Appendices are referred to:       a)    by the name of the species; or       b)    as being all of the species included in a higher taxon or designated part thereof.2.    The abbreviation “spp.” is used to denote all species of a higher taxon.3.    Other references to taxa higher than species are for the purposes of information or classification only. The common names included after the scientific names of families are for reference only. They are intended to indicate the species within the family concerned that are included in the Appendices.
