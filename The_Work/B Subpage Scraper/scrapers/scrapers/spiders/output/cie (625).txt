MD-715 provides a roadmap for creating effective equal employment opportunity (EEO) programs for all federal employees as required by Title VII and the Rehabilitation Act. MD-715 took effect on October 1, 2003. A copy of MD-715 and the Instructions are available on the EEOC’s web site: . Every year, the Department responds to the EEOC’s MD-715 in the form of a report.
