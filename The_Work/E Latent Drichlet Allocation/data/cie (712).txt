May be incomplete or contain other coding.This collection reprints key articles in the debate about the meaning of imperialism and colonialism from the 19th century to the present day. In papers by Thorsten Veblen, Max Weber, Keith Haynes, Gayatri Chakravorty Spivak, Homi K. Bhabha and other major scholars past and present, the set highlights the ways in which such concepts have changed their meaning over time.
