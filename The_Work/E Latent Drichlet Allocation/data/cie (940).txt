Corporate Profile
Roxgold is a Canadian-based gold mining company with assets located in West Africa.
The Company owns and operates the high-grade Yaramoko Mine Complex located on the Houndé greenstone belt in Burkina Faso and is also advancing the development and exploration of the Séguéla Gold Project located in Côte d’Ivoire.
