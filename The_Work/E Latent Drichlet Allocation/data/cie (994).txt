The No FEAR Act became effective on October 1, 2003.This act requires that federal agencies post on their public Web sites certain summary statistical data relating to equal employment opportunity complaints filed against the respective agencies.The State Department’s public website contains this statistical information in accordance with the No FEAR Act.
