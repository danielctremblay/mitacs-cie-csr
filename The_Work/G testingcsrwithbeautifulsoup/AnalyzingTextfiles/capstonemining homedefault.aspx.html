An Innovative Copper Producer Positioned for Transformational Growth

Capstone Mining is a base-metals producer with two producing copper mines: Pinto Valley in the US and Cozamin in Mexico. Capstone also owns 100% of Santo Domingo, a large scale, fully-permitted, copper-iron-gold project in Region III, Chile, as well as a portfolio of exploration properties.

More About Us

Why Invest

Transformational Growth

Pinto Valley’s PV3 Optimization, Copper Cathode Expansion and PV4 Expansion Study targeted to drive higher NPV, plus the fully-permitted copper-iron ore-gold Santo Domingo project with cobalt-optionality provides transformational growth opportunities

Exploration Excellence

NAV growth through the drill bit being delivered at Cozamin, with expected 50% copper and silver production growth in 2021+

Pioneering New Technology

Significant value to surface as copper is recovered from residual and high-grade waste at Pinto Valley using novel patented technology
