Our Story

New Pacific Metals is exploring and developing precious and base metals properties in Bolivia.

Our flagship property is the Silver Sand project in the Potosí Department of Bolivia.

 
 
 

Backed by strategic partners

Our largest shareholders are Silvercorp Metals Inc. (TSX:SVM), the largest primary silver producer in China, and Pan American Silver Corp. (TSX: PAAS), one of the world’s largest primary silver producers, which operates six mines, including the San Vicente mine located in the Potosí Department of Bolivia. 

 

The 2020 OTCQX Best 50 is an annual ranking of the top 50 U.S. and international companies traded on the OTCQX in 2019.

We were the number #1 performing stock on the OTCQX, which was based on total return and average daily dollar volume growth.
