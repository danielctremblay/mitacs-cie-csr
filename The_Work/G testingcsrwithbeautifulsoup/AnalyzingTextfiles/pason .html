Pason is a leading energy services and technology company. We develop and deliver high-value hardware, software, and services, primarily for the oil and gas drilling industry in 12 countries. Our motto is: Technology Deployed Simply.

Beyond drilling

Pason offers products and services for energy industry applications beyond drilling through Energy Toolbase and Verdazo.
