Our StoryNew Pacific Metals is exploring and developing precious and base metals properties in Bolivia.Our flagship property is the Silver Sand project in the Potosí Department of Bolivia. 







 















 Backed by strategic partnersOur largest shareholders are Silvercorp Metals Inc. (TSX:SVM), the largest primary silver producer in China, and Pan American Silver Corp. (TSX: PAAS), one of the world’s largest primary silver producers, which operates six mines, including the San Vicente mine located in the Potosí Department of Bolivia.
