Labrador Iron Ore Royalty Corporation

Labrador Iron Ore Royalty Corporation is a Canadian publicly traded company that provides investors exposure to the iron ore market through its royalty, commission and minority equity interests in Iron Ore Company of Canada, a leading North American producer and exporter of premium iron ore pellets and high-grade concentrate.
