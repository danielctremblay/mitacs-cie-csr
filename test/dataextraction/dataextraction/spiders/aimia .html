Aimia Inc. is a holding company with a focus on long-term investments in public and private companies, on a global basis, through controlling or minority stakes, targeting the deployment of its cash and tax losses towards the acquisitions of free cash flow generating businesses in diverse industries. The company consists of a lean corporate team and is guided by a highly aligned board to growing the value of its core holdings which includes PLM, Clear Media Limited, Kognitiv, BigLife, and Mittleman Investment Management.
