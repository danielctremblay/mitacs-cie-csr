Imperial Metals Corporation

is a Vancouver based mining company active in the acquisition, exploration, development, mining and production of base and precious metals. Our principal properties in British Columbia include the wholly owned Mount Polley and Huckleberry copper mines, a 30% interest in the Red Chris mine, and a 45.3% interest in the Ruddock Creek lead/zinc property.
