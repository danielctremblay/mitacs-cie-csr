About Us

Ritchie Bros. is the world's largest auctioneer of heavy equipment and trucks. We're the auction experts—and we've been helping people around the world buy and sell with confidence since 1958.

In 2018, Ritchie Bros. sold $4.96 billion of used equipment and other assets.  We offer a wide range of unused and used equipment and trucks for sale:
