Projects

Silver Elephant's 100% controlled Pulacayo silver-lead-zinc project (“Pulacayo project”) in Bolivia consists of the Pulacayo Deposit and Paca Deposit (7km north of Pulacayo deposit). The project is 107 km northeast of Sumitomo Corporation’s San Cristobal silver mine, 171 km southwest of New Pacific’s Silver Sands discovery, and 139 km north of Pan American Silver Corp.’s San Vicente silver mine.

Silver Elephant's 100% owned Minago Nickel Project is located in Thompson nickel belt (“TNB”), Manitoba, Canada. The Minago Project contains a sulphide nickel deposit (Nose deposit) with a historic resource. A qualified person as defined by NI 43-101 has not done sufficient work to classify the historical estimate as current mineral resources or mineral reserves.
